# LumvalePDF User Guide

Welcome to LumvalePDF, your offline, fast, and secure PDF editor!

## Getting Started

LumvalePDF is designed with a premium dark theme and native app feel. It runs fully offline, meaning your documents never leave your device.

### Opening a Document
1. Launch LumvalePDF.
2. Click **"Browse Files"** or drag and drop a PDF into the main window.
3. Your PDF will open instantly using our high-performance WebAssembly rendering engine.

### Reading and Navigating
- **Zooming**: Use the `Zoom In` and `Zoom Out` buttons in the toolbar. LumvalePDF pre-fetches page dimensions so zooming is completely smooth across all pages!
- **Sidebar**: Toggle the left sidebar to view **Thumbnails** or **Bookmarks**.
- **Scrolling**: Enjoy custom-themed scrollbars that match the application's aesthetic.

## Edit Mode

To prevent accidental changes while reading, LumvalePDF uses an explicit **Edit Mode**.
Click the **"Edit Document"** toggle on the toolbar to unlock the following features:

### Annotating Documents
1. With Edit Mode active, click the **Annotate** (pencil) icon.
2. An annotation toolbar will appear, giving you access to:
   - **Select Tool**: Move and resize existing annotations.
   - **Pen Tool**: Draw freehand. LumvalePDF uses a Catmull-Rom spline algorithm to ensure your handwriting and shapes are perfectly smooth.
   - **Highlighter**: Highlight important text.
   - **Text Tool**: Add typed text boxes anywhere.
3. Change colors and stroke widths using the toolbar options.

### Global Save & Download
Once you are done annotating, merging, or editing metadata, simply click the green **"Save & Download"** button in the top menu bar.

If you have pending annotations, LumvalePDF will prompt you with the **SaveModal**:
- **Native Annotations (Recommended)**: Saves your drawings and text as standard PDF annotations, meaning you can edit them later in LumvalePDF or Adobe Acrobat.
- **Flatten Document**: Permanently burns your annotations into the PDF pages. They will become part of the image and cannot be modified.

After making your choice, the final PDF will instantly download to your computer.

## Other Tools

- **Merge**: Add another PDF to your current document.
- **Extract**: Select specific pages from the thumbnails panel and download them as a new PDF.
- **Split**: Cut your PDF into multiple smaller files.
- **Compress**: Reduce the file size of your PDF while maintaining quality.
- **Watermark**: Stamp your document with a custom text watermark.
- **Metadata**: Edit the Author, Title, and Subject properties of the PDF.
- **Encrypt**: Secure your PDF with a password.

## Application Theming

LumvalePDF behaves like a native operating system window.
- The top title bar is custom-styled to match the app's dark background, seamlessly blending the window controls (Minimize/Maximize/Close) into the app.
- You can drag the application window directly by clicking and dragging the top navigation bar.

Enjoy using LumvalePDF!
